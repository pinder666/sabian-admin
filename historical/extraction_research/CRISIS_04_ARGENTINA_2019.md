# ARGENTINA 2019 — EXTRACTION MAP

## CRISIS SUMMARY
Argentina's ninth sovereign default. May 22, 2020: default after 30-day grace period expired on $65B in dollar-denominated bonds. By September 2020, restructured $66B foreign-law debt (95% bondholder approval) + $41B domestic-law debt.

## STRATEGIC POSITIONING (Pre-Stress)

### IMF
- **2018**: $57B stand-by arrangement — largest in IMF history
- **Pattern**: Emergency lending during Macri government, inherited debt trap for successor

---

## INSTITUTIONAL EXIT / RESTRUCTURING

### Major Bondholders
- **2020 restructuring**: 95% bondholder participation
- Large institutional holders accepted haircut rather than litigate

### The 2016 Settlement (2001 Default Legacy)
- **Elliott Management / NML Capital**: Refused 2005 and 2010 restructurings
- **2016 Settlement**: Argentina paid holdouts $4.65 billion total
- **Elliott's return**: Bought at estimated 20-25 cents, received $2.426B on face value — **1,270% return**

---

## DISTRESSED ENTRY — THE VULTURE PLAYBOOK

### Elliott Management / NML Capital
- **Strategy**: Buy post-default bonds at 10-30 cents, litigate for full repayment
- **2012**: Won US court ruling — pari passu clause meant Argentina couldn't pay anyone until it paid holdouts
- **Tactics**: 
  - Seized Argentine naval vessel in Ghana
  - Blocked Argentina from global capital markets for 15 years
- **Settlement (2016)**: $2.426B on ~$177M investment = **1,270% return**

### Aurelius Capital Management
- **Role**: Co-lead holdout with Elliott
- **2016**: Part of $4.65B settlement
- **2019**: Sued Argentina again over $84M in GDP-linked securities

### The Court Ruling
> "U.S. District Judge Thomas Griesa ordered Argentina to pay the holdouts, led by Elliott Management Corp's NML Capital Ltd and Aurelius Capital Management, who rejected the swaps and are fighting for full repayment in the courts."

## UNIQUE FEATURES

### The Pari Passu Precedent
- Elliott's legal strategy: argue "equal treatment" clause means holdouts must be paid before restructuring participants
- **Impact**: Created template for all future vulture fund litigation
- **Result**: Argentina blocked from capital markets for 15 years

### Repeat Offender
- Argentina's 9th default
- Same actors (Elliott) profited from multiple Argentine crises
