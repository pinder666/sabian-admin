# MOZAMBIQUE 2016 — EXTRACTION MAP

## CRISIS SUMMARY
Hidden debt scandal. $2B in secret loans from Credit Suisse and VTB revealed in 2016. Government officials bribed. IMF/World Bank froze support. Economic collapse. Two million pushed into poverty. Credit Suisse fined ~$500M globally.

## THE SCHEME

### 2013-2014: Loan Origination
- Three state-owned companies borrowed $2B
- Loans backed by undisclosed sovereign guarantees
- **Ostensible purpose**: Tuna fishing fleet, maritime security
- **Actual use**: Large portion went missing

### Key Actors
- **Privinvest** (Iskandar Safa): Shipbuilding company, contractor
- **Credit Suisse bankers**: Andrew Pearse, Surjan Singh, Detelina Subeva
- **Finance Minister Manuel Chang**: Received bribes

### Bribes Paid
- **$100M+** allegedly paid to Mozambique officials and Credit Suisse employees
- **$7M minimum** confirmed to Finance Minister Chang (court ruling)

---

## STRATEGIC POSITIONING (Pre-Stress)

### Credit Suisse
- Arranged and marketed the loans
- Bankers received personal kickbacks
- **Fine**: ~$500M (UK, US, European regulators combined)

### Privinvest
- Won contracts to build the fishing fleet
- Inflated pricing, missing deliverables
- Currently in legal appeals

### VTB (Russian State Bank)
- Co-lender, $535M exposure
- Limited public accountability

## VALUE TRANSFER MAGNITUDE

### Economic Impact
- **Lost output**: Entire year's GDP equivalent
- **People pushed into poverty**: 2 million
- **IMF/World Bank freeze**: Cut off access to concessional financing

### Financial Extraction
- **Bribes**: $100M+ to officials and bankers
- **Missing funds**: Large portion of $2B unaccounted for
- **Loan servicing**: Mozambique on hook despite fraud

### Court Ruling (2023)
- UK High Court ruled loans were procured by fraud and bribery
- Mozambique potentially relieved of repayment obligation on some debt

## UNIQUE FEATURES

### The "Tuna Bond" as Cover Story
- Loans marketed as development financing (fishing, maritime security)
- Actual use: corruption, missing funds
- International banks knowingly facilitated

### Triple Extraction
1. **Bribe extraction**: Officials and bankers took $100M+
2. **Contract extraction**: Privinvest overcharged for underdelivered fleet
3. **Debt extraction**: Mozambique left with $2B liability, lost IMF support

### Regulatory Response
- Credit Suisse fined ~$500M globally
- Bankers criminally charged
- Finance Minister extradited to US (2023)
