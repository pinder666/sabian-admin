# LEBANON 2020 — EXTRACTION MAP

## CRISIS SUMMARY
First sovereign default in Lebanon's history. Default on $1.2B Eurobond March 16, 2020 triggered cross-default on all $31.3B outstanding Eurobonds. Banking collapse, currency collapse, elite capital flight.

## INSTITUTIONAL EXIT — THE GREAT SELL-OFF

### Pre-Default Capital Flight
- **October 2019 onward**: ~$4 billion shipped out despite "capital controls"
- **November 2019**: Eurobond payment "immediately left the country in the absence of capital controls"
- **Pattern**: Insider exit, public frozen

### Banking Sector Eurobond Dump
- Banks sold billions in Eurobonds at deflated prices to foreign firms
- **Motivation**: "Narrow desire of top managers, big shareholders and ultra-wealthy depositors to gain fresh cash and transfer it abroad"
- **Pattern**: Insiders convert illiquid local bonds to liquid foreign cash, exit

## UNIQUE FEATURES

### Political Capture
- Banking sector and political elite deeply intertwined
- No distinction between "government" and "creditor" — same people

### Capital Controls Arbitrage
- Controls announced publicly, but elites moved money before announcement
- Two-tier system: insiders liquid, public frozen
