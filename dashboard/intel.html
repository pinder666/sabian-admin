Methodology
  • Findings persist to a hash-chained miner_findings table. Every row is timestamped and sealed weekly into a Bitcoin OpenTimestamps proof anchor.
  • Correlations are Spearman rank, computed across all country-year pairs where both signals have data. Minimum n = 30 to surface.
  • Going-dark windows are 2+ consecutive years where a signal value held flat at baseline, with score delta measured dark-start vs dark-end.
  • Lead indicators sweep lags 1–10 years. Where correlation strengthens at lag, signal A is treated as a precursor.
  • The miner runs nightly. Every claim is provable from a row ID against a sealed anchor.
