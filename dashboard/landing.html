The Design

Intelligence that shows its work.

Every score is a weighted average of live signals — computed in the open, readable by any analyst, reproducible from first principles.

01 — Observe

16 public data sources

ACLED conflict events. FEWS NET food security. NASA satellite fire. OONI internet shutdowns. OFAC sanctions. GPSJam interference. All open, all citable, all auditable.

02 — Score

One transparent equation

Weighted average. Null signals redistribute proportionally — no fabricated values injected. Every weight is labeled "judgment-set" and published, so any analyst can challenge them.

03 — Record

Timestamped observation ledger

Every threshold crossing is recorded with a decision window. When the window closes, the observation is graded: HIT, MISS, or PARTIAL. The hit rate is published. Honestly.
